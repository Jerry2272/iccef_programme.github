#StartupSouth instituted an award category known as Innocent Chukwuma Award for Innovation as a category within their Annual Award event. The award is awarded to an individual/organization in recognition of their contribution to the tech innovation ecosystem. The award was launched in September 2021.

Association for Research on Civil Society in Africa, AROCSA

Association for Research on Civil Society in Africa, AROCSA, is a regional association modelled after ARNOVA (Association for Research on Non-Profit Organizations & Voluntary Action). Innocent while in Ford Foundation as Resident Director had initiated the idea in 2015. AROCSA has an executive leadership transition programme for the founders/executive directors of NGOs planning transitions. In October 2021 after Innocent’s demise, AROCSA changed the name of the fellowship to Innocent Chukwuma African NGO Leadership Transition Fellowship Programme.

Innocent Chukwuma Social Entrepreneurship Prize for Youth and Gender Empowerment in Southeastern Nigeria.

This Annual Award in Innocent Chukwuma’s Honour was instituted by the board of LEAP Africa. The Social Innovators Programme & Awards started in September 2022. Click here to see the 2022 winner of the ICSEP

The ‘Innocent Chukwuma Social Impact Fellowship

The ‘Innocent Chukwuma Social Impact Fellowship’ is a capacity-building programme that is designed to support young Nigerians to lead transitions through their social change initiatives in communities, social enterprises, nonprofit organisations, research, and influence on development policy implementation. The fellowship equips the ten youth fellows with the necessary skills to deal strategically with complex challenges and opportunities in leading their organisations and communities, and it also gives the youth fellows the opportunity to create a network of local, national and international partners committed to sustainable development and feminist and responsible leadership principles. The fellowship runs for 6months annually. Ford Foundation supported the first Innocent Chukwuma Social Impact Chair & Fellowship (ICSICF) and it is being hosted by Lagos Business School.
